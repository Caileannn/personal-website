New Media Artist & Creative Technologist

Cailean Finn is a multi-disciplinary artist and creative technologist based in Ireland. His practice critically engages with technologies deeply embedded in our society, exposing underlying structures and hidden processes - exploring the relationship we have formed with machines and their wider ecological impact.

Current projects explore Artificial Intelligence tools and our relationship with the emerging technology.
