New Media Artist & Creative Technologist

Cailean.Finn is a New Media Artist based in Ireland. His artistic practice is centered around exploring the technologicl capabilities of various digital and analogous systems. By isolating the inherent limitations of a certain systsem, Cailean’s work aims to locate and embrace the hidden processes embedded within its limitations, and then, experimeting with the visual languages that emerge. The primary focus of his research and practice is situated around video processing, imaging technologies, and computer graphics - deriving inspiration from the relationships we have formed with technology and machines, and development made through the intersection of art, technology, and science.
