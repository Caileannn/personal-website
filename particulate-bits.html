Particulate Bits

#Installation

#Arduino

#Air Pollution

Particulate Bits is an investigation of the relationship between Air Pollution and us as individuals, primarily focusing on the negative effects that it can cause to our health and environments. The relationship is explored through two artworks created by using an Arduino that hopes to provide an engaging experience for the viewer. In an attempt to raise awareness for particular concerns regarding air pollution and environmental issues.

(Completed for the Physical Computing module during my M.A)
